Baked Salmon

Description

An easy-to-make, crispy, salmon.

Ingredients

  • 1lb salmon fillet
  • Epic Brand Pork Fat
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. 30 minutes before cooking, place the salmon on aluminum foil and cover lightly with salt
  2. After 30m of dry-brining, rinse the salmon with water to remove the salt and dry with a towel
  3. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  4. Coat the salmon lightly in pork fat
  5. Place the salmon skin-side down in the oven and bake for 20m
