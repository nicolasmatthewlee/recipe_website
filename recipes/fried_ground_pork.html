Fried Ground Pork

Description

A simple and enjoyable dish that can be prepared in 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Epic Brand Pork Fat
  • 1lb duBreton Ground Pork

Instructions

  1. Set a frying pan over the stove and set to medium-high heat
  2. Place approximately 1tbsp pork fat in the frying pan
  3. Take the ground pork out of the refrigerator and flatten to about 1cm height
  4. Once the pan is lightly smoking, place the pork over the pan and cover with a lid
  5. After 1m, use the spatula to break up the cooking pork into smaller pieces, about 1-4sq inches in area
  6. Cook for approximately 5-6m, or until it starts to smoke, and then flip for another 3-4m
